FAQ

  • Can I use CRMPP for free?
    • Yes, we offer the basic features of CRMPP for free. However, you won't be able to use other advanced tools and features unless on a paid subscription.
  • New to CRMPP?
    • Welcome to CRMPP, your ultimate CRM partner! You may read the following to get started:
    1. Browse your Knowledge Base to find topics you might be interested in.
    2. Read our user's manual guide, which is found at the bottom of FAQs Page, to learn more about CRMPP.
    3. Visit your dashboard to delegate tasks, find sales leads, and integrate marketing strategies.
    For more questions about how to use CRMPP, feel free to leave us a message or send an email.
  • What are the different subscription plans?
    • CRMPP has three subscription plans: Free, Standard, and Premium. Visit our Plans and Pricing page to view all the benefits included in each plan.
  • Can I change my subscription plan?
    • You may do so by following these steps:
    1. Go to the CRMPP settings and click 'Account Details'.
    2. Upon reaching 'Account Details', select 'Change Plan' and choose your new subscription plan.
    3. Enter your credit card information and select 'update plan'.
    4. Confirm the changes.
  • Can I cancel my CRMPP account?
    • Yes, any time. Just follow these steps:
    1. Go to the CRMPP settings and select 'Account Details'.
    2. Choose 'Cancel CRMPP Account' found at the bottom of the Account Details page.
    3. Confirm the cancellation of your account.
    Before deleting your CRMPP account, export all your existing data and file. Once the account is deleted, the data or file is no longer accessible.
  • How does CRMPP work?
    • Upon creating a CRMPP account, you can mobilize workforce, delegate tasks, integrate emails, manage business, and monitor sales goals.
  • How do I add or remove users?
    • Visit the user's profile and click 'remove user'. The user will be automatically removed from your database and our system.
  • How do I import data into CRMPP?
    • Go to the data section of the dashboard. You can either drag and drop the data, or browse for the file.
  • How do I export data from CRMPP?
    • Select the file to be exported, right click, and choose 'export the file' or 'save the link as'.
  • How do I oversee sales goals and marketing campaigns?
    • Go to your CRMPP account and visit your dashboard. For sales, click the 'sales' tab. From there, you can find your sales leads and goals, or assign sales targets to each user (salesperson). For marketing, go to the 'marketing' tab. By doing so, you can delegate marketing schemes to each user (marketing associate) and modify strategies.
  • How can I create an email template?
    • Visit the email section of your CRMPP account and click 'Settings'. Next, click to 'General' tab and enter your email template.
  • How can I manage activities, events, and tasks?
    • For activities and events, visit the 'activity' section of your dashboard. You can create, modify, or remove a particular activity or event. To do so, click the specific activity or event and click 'edit'.

    For tasks, go to the 'task' portion. You can create, edit, or remove a certain task by clicking 'edit'.
